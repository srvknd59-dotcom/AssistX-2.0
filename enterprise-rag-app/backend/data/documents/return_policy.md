# Northwind Gadgets — Return & Warranty Policy

## Return Window
Products purchased directly from northwindgadgets.example may be
returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided the
item is unused and in its original packaging.

## Warranty
All Northwind Gadgets products include a 1-year limited warranty
covering manufacturing defects. The warranty does not cover damage
from drops, water exposure beyond the product's rating, or
unauthorized repairs.

## How to Start a Return
1. Log into your account and go to "Order History."
2. Select the order and click "Start a Return."
3. Print the prepaid shipping label and attach it to the package.
4. Drop the package off at any partner carrier location.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business
days after the returned item is received and inspected.

## Exchanges
Exchanges for a different color or size follow the same process as a
return, but you can select "Exchange" instead of "Refund" at checkout
to avoid waiting for the refund before the replacement ships.
