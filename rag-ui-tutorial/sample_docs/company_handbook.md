# Northwind Gadgets — Employee Handbook (excerpt)

## Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM, Monday through Friday.
Employees may request flexible hours by speaking with their manager;
approved flexible schedules must still cover the core hours of
11:00 AM to 3:00 PM so teams can meet.

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work requests
for more than 3 days require director approval and are reviewed quarterly.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)
Full-time employees accrue 15 days of PTO per year during their first
two years, increasing to 20 days after two years of service. PTO
requests should be submitted at least 5 business days in advance
through the HR portal.

## Expense Reimbursement
Employees can submit expense reports for approved business costs
(travel, client meals, conference fees) within 30 days of the expense.
Reimbursements are processed within 10 business days of approval.

## IT Support
For IT issues, employees should open a ticket through the internal
helpdesk. Standard response time is within 1 business day; urgent
issues (systems down) are addressed within 2 hours during business hours.
