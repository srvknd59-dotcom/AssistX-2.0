# Northwind Gadgets — GlowMug Smart Mug FAQ

## What is the GlowMug?
The GlowMug is a temperature-controlled smart mug that keeps drinks
between 130°F and 145°F for up to 3 hours on a single charge. It
connects to the Northwind app over Bluetooth to let you set your
preferred temperature.

## How do I charge it?
The GlowMug ships with a USB-C charging coaster. Place the mug on the
coaster to charge; a full charge from empty takes about 90 minutes.
The mug will beep twice when charging starts and once when complete.

## Is the GlowMug dishwasher safe?
The mug body is hand-wash only because it contains internal heating
electronics. The lid is dishwasher safe on the top rack.

## Why won't my GlowMug connect to the app?
1. Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone.
2. Hold the button on the mug's base for 5 seconds until the light
   flashes blue — this puts it into pairing mode.
3. Open the Northwind app and select "Add Device."
4. If it still fails, restart your phone's Bluetooth and try again.

## What is the battery life?
On a full charge, the GlowMug maintains temperature for approximately
3 hours of active use, or up to 8 hours in standby (lid closed, not
actively heating).
